About Us

"Gift of Life" is a community-driven platform designed to connect voluntary blood donors with those in urgent need. Our mission is to save lives by making blood donation accessible, efficient, and transparent.

How It Works

Register

Create an account as a donor or recipient.

Search

Find available donors near your location or based on blood group.

Connect

Communicate and schedule blood donation appointments.

Did You Know?

  • 💉 One donation can save up to three lives.
  • 🕒 It only takes about 10 minutes to donate blood.
  • 💖 Blood cannot be manufactured—it can only come from donors.
